Endless Poetry

This tremor, this bewilderment, this wandering towards the depths of the faraway and never. I lost my mountains and their sharp scent. I lost my ocean We have always been mute! I hear the woes of the world. "I am but my own absence" behind a broken mirror. Who's there? The party thrower! Just a minute. What do you want? I want to be your friend. Are you homosexual? No. Then why do you want to be my friend? Because I admire your poetry. "The death I flee runs slowly beside me." There are even poems on the floor! This wonderful work will be lost. Everything will vanish. Our souls will disappear. It doesn't matter. Dreams vanish too, and little by little we dissolve. Poetry, like the shadow of a flying eagle, leaves no trace on land. A poem reaches perfection when it burns. I couldn't bear to see this work of art burn. by giving me nothing you gave me everything. Better to be dead. Better to not have been born. Enough, Enrique. You're destroying yourself. We won't let you kill yourself. Nothingness is in everything. You're wasting your time. Life is in everything. Life. It isn't you suffering. It's the image you've made of yourself. You're a poet! Perceive reality differently. I hear the woes of the world.
